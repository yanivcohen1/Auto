Favourite Products and Pinned Builds

Favourites

What does Favouriting a Product do?

Favouriting a product moves it YOUR favourites section on the product page. It allows quick access to the products you use most frequently.

How to Favourite a Product

Favouriting a product is very simple, simply click on the settings cog next to a product

And then click on the "Favourite" option

How to Un-Favourite Product

This is the same process as favouriting because the button acts as a toggle. Just this time when you open the settings cog the Favourite option will already be highlighting. Clicking it again will unhighlight it.

Pinned Builds

What does Pinning a Build do?

Pinning a build moves it to the pinned section on EVERYONE'S view. This is used to mark a build for others to see so they can access the same build as you quicker.

How to Pin a Build

To Pin a build open the build menu and click on the settings cog next to a build

Then choose the "Pin Build" option

It will appear in the pinned section

How to Un-Pin a Build

Un-Pinning a build is exactly the same process as the button is actually a toggle, simply click on the settings cog of the build you wish to un-pin and choose the "Un-Pin Build" option
