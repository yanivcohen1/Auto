pages with your Skype contacts directly from Chrome and Firefox browsers. If you are a Skype Click to Call user, you can continue to call phone numbers on websites with one click, as usual. <br /> We will be adding more features and supporting more browsers in the future.<br /> <br /> To access the full Skype functionality, use the Skype Web Client available at <a href="https://web.skype.com/"><font color="#0000ff">https://web.skype.com</font></a>. Audio and Video calling on Skype Web Client is currently not supported on Chrome OS and Linux. </p> <h3> How do I use the Skype extension for sharing to Skype?</h3> <p> Sharing content from websites to your Skype network is as easy as clicking on the extension. To start sharing, follow these steps:</p> <ol> <li> Find a webpage you want to share and click on the Skype extension installed in your browser. <br /> <br /> <img src="https://az705183.vo.msecnd.net/onlinesupportmedia/onlinesupport/media/skype/screenshots/fa34612/fa34612_1.png" alt="" /></li> <li> If you're not already signed in to Skype in your browser, a pop-up or new tab will appear that will prompt you to sign in. <br /> <br /> <img src="https://az705183.vo.msecnd.net/onlinesupportmedia/onlinesupport/media/skype/screenshots/fa34612/fa34612_2.png" alt="" /></li> <li> Once signed in, you will see the share screen: <br /> <br /> <img src="https://az705183.vo.msecnd.net/onlinesupportmedia/onlinesupport/media/skype/screenshots/fa34612/fa34612_3.png" alt="" /><br /> <br /> <strong>A.</strong> A preview of the webpage that you are sharing<br /> <strong>B.</strong> The text box for you to add a message<br /> <strong>C.</strong> The search field to find your Skype contacts or recent conversations you'd like to share with<br /> <strong>D.</strong> The list of your Skype contacts and conversations</li> <li> Pick one or more contacts you would like to share the webpage with, or use the search field to find your Skype contacts if you don’t see them in the list. When you're ready, click the <strong>Send </strong>button. The webpage will be shared with the contacts you selected and the pop-up or tab will close automatically.</li> <li> If you have multiple Skype accounts and would like to share using a different account, please click on your avatar in the top right on the desktop browser or click on the <strong>Signed in as</strong> link at the top on the mobile browser. You can click on <strong>My Account</strong> to access your account or click on <strong>Sign out</strong> to sign out. <br /> <br /> <img src="https://az705183.vo.msecnd.net/onlinesupportmedia/onlinesupport/media/skype/screenshots/fa34612/fa34612_4.png" alt="" /></li> </ol> <p></p> </div> </div> </div> </div> </section> <section class="feedbackForm thinner"> <div class="centerWrapper"> <div class="showGrid grid verticalGutterDesktop horizontalGutterDesktop horizontalGutter container"> <div class="row"> <div class="column unit12"> <div class="feedbackContainer"> <div data-feedback-url="https://helperapi.skype.com/api/v1/feedback/" id="feedbackForm"> <input type="hidden" value="FA34612" name="faq_id" /> <div class="ctaContainer"> <span class="title"> <h3> Was this article helpful?
